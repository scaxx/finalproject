5 Seconds of Summer

5 Seconds Of Summer (also known as 5SOS) is a band originating from Sydney, Australia, in the pop rock genre, officially formed on December 3, 2011. They began to garner attention in 2012 after posting a series of covers of popular songs on YouTube.

5 Seconds of Summer released their first single, "Out of My Limit," exclusively in Australia and New Zealand in November 2012. In February 2014, they released "She Looks So Perfect" as their first worldwide release.

The band spans genres such as alternative rock, pop rock, power pop, pop, and pop punk.

Imagine Dragons

Imagine Dragons is an American pop rock band originating from Las Vegas, Nevada. They gained worldwide recognition with the release of their debut studio album Night Visions (2012), and with their song "It's Time." Billboard placed them at the top of their 2013 "Year In Rock" popularity list, calling them "the breakthrough band of 2013."

Active since 2010 and continuing to the present day, the band has encompassed genres such as pop rock, electro rock, alternative rock, and indie rock.
