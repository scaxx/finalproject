5 Seconds of Summer

A little more information:

5 Seconds of Summer is a pop-rock band originating from Sydney, Australia, officially created on December 3, 2011. It consists of Luke Hemmings, Michael Clifford, Calum Hood, and Ashton Irwin.

They gained recognition in 2012 after posting covers of popular songs on YouTube. Their first single, "Out of My Limit," was released in November 2012. They achieved worldwide success with "She Looks So Perfect" in February 2014.

They have released several successful albums, including "5 Seconds of Summer" (2014), "Sounds Good Feels Good" (2015), "Youngblood" (2018), and "Calm" (2020). Their latest album, "5SOS5," was released in September 2022.

They have embarked on worldwide tours, including the "Rock Out with Your Socks Out Tour" and the "Sounds Live Feels Live World Tour." They have performed at major events such as Fire Fight Australia in 2020.

Their music is characterized by a blend of pop-rock and pop-punk. Citing influences such as McFly, Blink-182, All Time Low, and Green Day, they aim to create a distinctive and authentic sound.

The band founded their own record label, Hi Or Hey Records, and have signed other bands like Hey Violet.

In summary, 5 Seconds of Summer has experienced an impressive rise to fame since their formation in 2011, establishing themselves as one of the most successful pop-rock bands of the last decade. Their catchy music and energetic live performances have made them favorites of a wide audience worldwide.
